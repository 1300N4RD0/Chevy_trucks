What type of truck builds can you make

the two main different types of builds when it comes to trucks are lowered vs lifted trucks

lifted trucks

What are lifted trucks? Lifted trucks are what they are lifted trucks you can turn any truck into a lifted truck with a lift kit and if you wanted to you could make your truck wider too these are more for offroading

Lowered trucks

Lowered trucks are just trucks that are lowered with a drop kit you can do this for drag racing or just for preference although this mod basically turns your truck into a sports car.
